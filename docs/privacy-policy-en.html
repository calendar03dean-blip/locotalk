🔒 Privacy Policy

Locotalk · Last updated: June 6, 2026

Locotalk ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

a. Account Registration & Login

Login MethodData Collected
GoogleEmail address, OAuth token
KakaoKakao account identifier, OAuth token
NaverNaver account identifier, OAuth token
EmailEmail address (for verification only)

b. During App Use

  • Nickname — Up to 6 characters, visible only to your matched neighbor
  • Interests — Up to 3 selections, used for matching
  • Location — GPS used to determine district-level area only (e.g., Mapo-gu); precise coordinates are not stored
  • Device Token — FCM/APNs token for push notifications
  • Contacts (optional · Premium) — Only when you enable "Avoid Matching Acquaintances," phone numbers from your contacts are converted into one-way cryptographic hashes (SHA-256). Raw phone numbers, names, and other contact details are never stored or transmitted; hashes are used solely to exclude acquaintances from matching.
💬 Chat messages are retained for a limited period (Free: 7 days, Premium: 90 days) to provide the service, then automatically deleted. Identifying information is erased immediately upon account deletion.
📇 "Avoid Matching Acquaintances" (Premium) compares contact phone numbers using cryptographic hashes only. Raw phone numbers and names are never collected, stored, or transmitted, and stored hashes are deleted immediately when you turn the feature off.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Identity verification and account management
  • Neighbor matching and anonymous chat services
  • Location-based neighborhood feed features
  • Avoid Matching Acquaintances — excluding people in your contacts from matching (Premium, via phone-number hash comparison)
  • Push notifications related to service usage
  • Prevention of illegal or inappropriate use

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When you have given prior consent
  • When required by law or government authorities

5. Social Login Providers

When using social login, the respective provider's privacy policy also applies:

  • Google — policies.google.com/privacy
  • Kakao — kakao.com/policy/privacy

6. Location Information

  • Location is only collected while the app is in use and can be denied at any time in device settings.
  • GPS coordinates are converted to a district name and immediately discarded.
  • Precise coordinates are never stored on our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

This service is available only to adults aged 19 and older, and registration by anyone under 19 is not permitted. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

8. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Delete your account (via "My Profile → Start Over" in the app)
